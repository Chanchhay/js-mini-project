About Cambodia's Temples

Khmer Heritage

The temples of Cambodia represent one of the world's most remarkable architectural achievements. Built during the height of the Khmer Empire (9th-15th centuries), these sacred sites showcase the incredible skill and devotion of ancient builders.

From the iconic Angkor Wat to the mysterious faces of Bayon, each temple tells a unique story of religious devotion, architectural innovation, and artistic mastery that continues to inspire visitors from around the world.
